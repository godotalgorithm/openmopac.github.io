---
title: "Guides"
description: "Guides for common MOPAC usage"
---

MOPAC has accumulated a lot of functionality in its over four decades of development,
and its online manual contains detailed information about all of its functionality and many intended use cases.
However, most usage of MOPAC recorded in the scientific literature is concentrated on a relatively limited set of functionality.
These guides are intended to help users learn the proper usage of MOPAC's most popular features as well as a few of MOPAC's novel but underutilized features.
