# **M**olecular **O**rbital **PAC**kage

Enabling simple, low-cost quantum chemistry calculations since 1983.

- Semiempirical models such as AM1, PM6, and PM7
- COSMO model for implicit solvation
- MOZYME solver for fast closed-shell calculations
- Gamma-point treatment of periodic systems in 1D, 2D, and 3D
